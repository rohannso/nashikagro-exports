Who We Are

Rooted in Niphad,
Trusted Worldwide

Sai Prasad Marketing is a Nashik-based fruits and vegetable export company headquartered in Pimpalgeon Baswant, Niphad — the heart of India's onion and grape belt.

Founded by Sanskar Shankar Deokar, we have over 10 years of hands-on export experience, building direct relationships with farmers and international buyers across the UAE, Europe, and Southeast Asia.
