Who We Are

Rooted in Nashik,
Reaching the World

We are a Nashik-based agricultural export company with direct partnerships with local farmers across the region. Nashik's unique climate — blessed with the Godavari basin and rich basaltic soil — produces some of India's finest fruits and vegetables.

Our end-to-end process covers procurement, quality grading, packaging, phytosanitary certification, customs documentation, and cold-chain logistics — ensuring your products arrive fresh, compliant, and on time.
